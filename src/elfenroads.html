Elfenroads

Unity
Photon

Elfenroads is turn-based a strategy game where players must travel to as many towns as possible throughout several rounds, while accounting for transportation types, terrain, and various obstacles. The mechanics of the game allow for creative plays that require a lot of foresight, keeping every round fresh and interesting.

This project was made by a team of 7 students over a 9 month period. My responsibility was mainly to implement core gameplay mechanics, such as card playing, terrain types, and traveling. Since this is a mechanically heavy game, combined with the fact that we have implemented it to be online multiplayer, there were many edge cases and possible bugs to tackle. Therefore development was tedious at times, but working on a long term project with a team was an extremely valuable learning experience.
